About Krunker.io

Krunker.io is a fast-paced pixel-style FPS (First Person Shooter) IO game that offers intense multiplayer action. With its unique movement mechanics like slide hopping, customizable characters, and various game modes, Krunker.io provides an exciting competitive gaming experience for players of all skill levels.

Key Features:

  • Fast-paced FPS gameplay with unique movement mechanics
  • Multiple character classes with different abilities
  • Customizable weapons and skins
  • Various game modes including FFA, Team Deathmatch, and more
  • Community-driven with custom maps and mods
